  Our Aim

At E-Cell SIT, we assert that there exists a deficiency in exposure to the societal aspects of entrepreneurship, or entrepreneurship in general, within Engineering College. Even if there are students harboring specific ideas they wish to pursue, the prevailing culture and demanding timetable of Engineering college might stifle the inclination to embark on something new. The fundamental inquiry that arises is:'Where to Commence?' A widely held notion characterizes us as 'The Leaders for Tomorrow'. E-Cell, however, subscribes to the concept of being 'The Leaders for Today'. Our objective is to nurture the requirements of youthful intellects and their concepts while actively seeking transformative change. As aspiring entrepreneurs originating from legal backgrounds, we endeavor to advocate for a culture of embracing risk and the associated principles, with the aspiration of effecting meaningful change. The overarching goal is to establish an environment conducive to the acquisition of entrepreneurial knowledge, particularly tailored for individuals with an exclusive legal background.
